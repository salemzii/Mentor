Our Scholarship Packages

Our Scholarship Programmes are divided into Subcategories. In Order to fit the needs of several classes of people amongst us, as it is said, “Everyone, irrespective of their backgrounds deserves education”. For each categories listed below, an Application form is required to be filled, the form which contains Applicant’s details like date of birth, age, home address, link to school leaving certificate and other trivial informations. The Form cost 40 US Dollar to purchase and on filling, the Applicant is required to submit the form back for processing. This year we are sponsoring over 6,000 students to study abroad, thanks to the poverty alleviation scheme sponsored by Africans In Diaspora, we aim on educating 5% of talented students across the world that cannot access appropriate education. This year we feature several Institutions offering a variety of courses covering a broad field of knowledge, such as; Utrecht University, University of Nicosia, University of Lille, across countries like the Netherlands, Cyprus, France, and Germany.
